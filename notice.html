Deposit information

  • Please make a deposit within two days of the order. The payment confirmation is made 24 hours a day.
  • If the deposit is confirmed before 2 p.m., it will be sent on the same day only for products in stock.
  • The deposit after 2 p.m. will be confirmed and sent the next day. However, we do not ship the product because we are closed regularly on weekends.

Delivery Information

  • A shipping fee of 2,500 won will be added when purchasing.
  • Jeju Island and mountainous areas will cost an additional 4,000 won.
  • Pouvior uses convenience store courier service.
  • If the product is in stock, it will be shipped on the same day and you can receive it within a day or two.
